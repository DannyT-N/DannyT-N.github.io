About

I'm a Computer Science major at Howard University, originally from Newark, NJ, and a graduate of St. Paul's School (Class of 2024). I'm working toward a career in software engineering, with a longer-term interest in product management — I care as much about why something's worth building as I do about building it well.

This summer I'm putting that into practice as a Data Analyst Intern with the City of Newark's Office of Information Technology, working with real municipal data and systems. In past summers, I canvassed door-to-door for the Obama Foundation across North Jersey, raising support for the My Brother's Keeper Alliance and the Girls Opportunity Alliance — work that taught me as much about clear communication as any class has.

Outside of that, I've spent over eight years mentoring middle schoolers through a Newark-based nonprofit, and picked up golf through Howard's FairWays to Leadership program.
